Built for the Bow Valley

A local app for events, plans, people, and notices

Summit Scene brings together the pieces people usually have to hunt for separately: what is happening, who wants to make plans, who is new in town, and what locals need to know.

What it helps with

One place for local plans and events

Events

Find what is happening

Browse local events, save what looks interesting, and use the map to see what is nearby.

Plans

Make a plan

Post when you want someone to join a hike, coffee, ski day, walk, event, or casual meetup.

People

Meet newcomers

New in town, here for the season, or just open to saying hello? Summit Scene makes introductions easier.

Community side

Browse by what you actually need

Community is organized around simple paths: Make a Plan, New in Town, Groups, and Town Notices. That keeps browsing clear without mixing every post into one confusing feed.

For organizers

Businesses and event hosts can be found locally

Summit Scene supports business and organizer profiles so official events, venues, and experiences can appear where locals and visitors are already browsing.

Safety

Built with community controls

  • Public-place reminders for first plans.
  • Profile checks before meeting new people.
  • Report and block tools for posts, replies, and profiles.
  • Community guidelines and account review flows.
