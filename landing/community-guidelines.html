Community Guidelines

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Keep It Local, Useful, and Respectful

Summit Scene is for local events, plans, introductions, groups, town notices, and community discovery around Banff, Canmore, Lake Louise, and nearby areas.

Not Allowed

  • Harassment, hate, threats, bullying, or unwanted sexual content.
  • Scams, spam, misleading event details, fake business listings, or impersonation.
  • Illegal activity, unsafe instructions, or content encouraging harm.
  • Private personal information posted without permission.
  • Repeated off-topic posts or behavior that makes the app harder to use.

Events and Business Posts

  • Only post official business or organizer content if you are authorized.
  • Use clear event details, dates, locations, and expectations.
  • Do not imply permits, venue approval, or official approval unless you have it.

Reporting and Blocking

Use report or block tools when something feels unsafe, misleading, abusive, or inappropriate. Summit Scene may review reports and take action where needed.
