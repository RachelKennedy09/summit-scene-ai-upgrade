Safety

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Meet Thoughtfully

  • Meet in public places for first plans.
  • Tell someone where you are going and who you are meeting.
  • Keep first meetups simple and low-pressure.
  • Trust your judgment and leave any situation that feels uncomfortable.

Check Before You Go

  • Review profiles, event details, location, date, and time before making plans.
  • Confirm outdoor conditions, road conditions, weather, skill level, and required gear yourself.
  • For official events, check the host or venue if details matter for safety, access, tickets, or permits.

Use App Safety Tools

Report posts, replies, profiles, or business listings that seem unsafe, abusive, misleading, or inappropriate. Block users when you do not want to see their content or interactions.
