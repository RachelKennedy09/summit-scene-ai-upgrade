Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Delete Your Account in the App

When available, open Summit Scene, go to Account, and use the account deletion option. This is the fastest way to request deletion from inside the app.

Request Deletion by Email

You can also email hello@summitscene.ca with the subject line "Delete my Summit Scene account". Include the email address connected to your account.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile and login-associated app data.
  • Your public content where deletion is technically and operationally possible.
  • Saved events, interest signals, and app-specific preferences.

Limited Retention

Some information may be retained for a limited time when needed for security, abuse prevention, dispute handling, backups, legal compliance, or platform review requirements.
