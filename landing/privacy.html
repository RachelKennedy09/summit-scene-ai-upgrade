Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

This draft is for launch preparation and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before public release.

Overview

Summit Scene collects information needed to create accounts, show local events, support community features, review business accounts, moderate reports, and keep the app working.

Information We Collect

  • Account information, such as name or business name, email, password hash, role, town, profile details, avatar, social links, and business verification status.
  • Public content, such as event posts, community posts, plan posts, replies, profile information, likes, interests, attendance choices, and saved event choices.
  • Optional location data, if you grant permission, to help show nearby events and map features.
  • Safety and moderation data, including reports, blocks, moderation notes, and admin review actions.
  • Technical and support data, such as request logs, errors, support emails, and bug reports.

How We Use Information

  • To provide accounts, profiles, events, community posts, maps, replies, saved events, and plan features.
  • To review business and organizer accounts.
  • To respond to reports, block requests, moderation issues, support requests, and bug reports.
  • To improve app safety, reliability, and performance.
  • To comply with legal, safety, and platform review obligations.

Service Providers

We may use service providers for hosting, database storage, address lookup or geocoding, authentication, social login, analytics, support, and app operations. These providers process information only as needed to support Summit Scene.

Public Content

Profiles, events, community posts, replies, business information, and similar content may be visible to other users. Do not post private information you do not want others to see.

Retention and Deletion

You can request account and data deletion. Deletion removes the account and associated app data, except where limited retention is needed for security, abuse prevention, dispute handling, backups, or legal compliance.

Children

Summit Scene is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law or app store rules. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us.

Contact

Privacy questions and deletion requests can be sent to hello@summitscene.app.
